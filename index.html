The Simpsons

Short Description:

The series depicts the lives of the Simpson family—Homer, Marge, Bart, Lisa, and Maggie—in a satirical way. The family adventures take place in the fictional town of Springfield. The episodes include numerous references to American popular culture, politics, and other current phenomena, as well as problems within American society.

The setting of the series is the fictional American town of Springfield. The events focus on the Simpson family's home at 742 Evergreen Terrace, Springfield's elementary school, Moe's Tavern, and the Springfield nuclear power plant. Springfield's landscape includes coastlines, deserts, countryside, and high mountain peaks. Groening himself has stated that the town shares similarities with Portland, Oregon, the city where he grew up. The name Springfield was chosen for its commonality.
