Apu Nahasapeemapetilon, Jr.

Apu is the operator of the Kwik-E-Mart. He is a recurring character in The Simpsons. He is an immigrant from India , and as such, speaks with a strong Indian accent. Accounts disagree as to whether he is a college dropout or if he has a PhD.
